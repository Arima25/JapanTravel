Japanese Food Guide

Explore iconic dishes and find top restaurants on your travels!

Sushi

The most famous Japanese dish: vinegared rice topped with fresh seafood.

Ramen

Flavorful noodles in a variety of broths, topped with sliced pork and egg.

Tempura

Lightly battered seafood and vegetables, deep fried and served with dip.

Sashimi

Fresh slices of raw fish served with wasabi and soy sauce.

Soba

Delicate buckwheat noodles, served cold or in hot broth.
