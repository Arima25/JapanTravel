The wide varity of food in Japan

Japanese cuisine offers an enormous amount of delight accompany with a boundless variety of regional and seasonal dishes. There are many different kind of restrant in Japan ranging from mobile food stands to centuries old ryotei, atmospheric drinking places, seasonally erected terraces over rivers, cheap chain shops.

Some of the most popular food in japan are:

  • Soba
  • Sushi
  • Tempura
  • Sashimi
  • Ramen

As Japan is divided into many distinct geographic regions, there are some foods that you can only find in certain reigon and each reigon has developed its own unique culinary traditions.
